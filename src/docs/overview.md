# What this is

This application is an interactive, CSV-driven intelligence surface for mapping OEM system stacks, supplier dependencies, governance posture, and ecosystem relationships.

## How to read it

- **Grid** shows a structured matrix of OEM groups by layer.
- **Network** shows how entities connect across regions and categories.
- **Architecture** shows the stack as system lanes, rather than as a loose graph.
- **Governance** shows verification coverage and metadata quality.
- **Docs** explains method, evidence handling, and interpretation rules.

## Why supplier relationships matter

Supplier relationships are not decoration. They shape execution risk, operating leverage, bargaining power, dependency concentration, and the real boundary between OEM control and ecosystem control.
